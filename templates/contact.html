Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you

SnuggleStream is a small, independent project and we genuinely read every message that comes in. Whether you've found a bug, have a feature idea, need help with something, or just want to tell us about your movie night — drop us a line.

Email

The best way to reach us is by email:

contactus@snugglestream.uk

What You Can Contact Us About

  • Bug reports. Something broken or behaving oddly? Let us know what happened, which browser you're using, and any steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots help a lot.
  • Feature requests. Got an idea that would make SnuggleStream better? We're all ears. We can't promise we'll build everything, but we track every suggestion.
  • Privacy & data requests. Need to exercise your data rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA? Want to know what data we hold about you? Send us an email and we'll respond as quickly as possible.
  • DMCA / copyright complaints. If you believe content shared through SnuggleStream infringes your copyright, please contact us with details and we will take appropriate action promptly.
  • General feedback. Compliments, complaints, questions, or just saying hello — all welcome.
  • Partnership & press enquiries. If you'd like to write about SnuggleStream or discuss a collaboration, we'd love to chat.

Response Times

We're a small team (very small — think "two people and a pixelated cat"), so please allow up to 48 hours for a response. We try to reply much sooner than that, but sometimes life, work, and time zones get in the way. We will always get back to you.

Community

If you enjoy SnuggleStream and want to support the project, the best thing you can do is tell a friend. You can also buy us a coffee — it helps keep the servers running and the cat fed.
