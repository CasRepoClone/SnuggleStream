1. What SnuggleStream Is

SnuggleStream is a casual, social platform that lets friends watch video content together in real time. It synchronises playback so everyone in a room sees the same moment at the same time. That's it — we are not a hosting service, content provider, or distribution network.

2. Acceptable Use

You may use SnuggleStream to:

  • Watch content you already have legal access to — your own files, content you've purchased, or free / publicly available videos.
  • Share a private room with friends for personal, non-commercial viewing sessions.

3. Prohibited Conduct

You agree not to use SnuggleStream to:

  • Distribute pirated content. Do not upload, stream, or share links to copyrighted movies, TV shows, music, or any other media you do not have the right to distribute. This includes sharing pirated MP4 files, torrented media, or ripped streams.
  • Circumvent streaming service restrictions. Do not use SnuggleStream to bypass single-screen, single-account, or concurrent-stream limits imposed by subscription services (e.g. Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, Amazon Prime Video, etc.). If your subscription is licensed for one viewer, watching through SnuggleStream with others violates those terms — and ours.

  • Share or sell access to rooms for commercial gain. SnuggleStream is free and casual. Running paid screenings or selling room codes is not permitted.

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. We store only your display name and profile picture to show in rooms. We do not sell your data. Room data (codes, playback state) is held in memory and is not persisted — when a room empties, it's gone.
